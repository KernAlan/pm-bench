## How scores are computed

Each reported score is the mean of N iterations against the deterministic scoring rubric in `run.py`. 95% CI uses the normal approximation 1.96 × std / sqrt(N). Submissions with N=1 are shown but not directly comparable in confidence — three or more iterations are recommended.

Open-ended mode uses an LLM judge. The reported judge model is whichever the submitter passed via `--judge-model`. Submissions where subject and judge are the same model family should be flagged as potentially self-biased.

## How to submit

1. Run `python run.py [--mode open-ended] [--superhuman-only]` at least 3 iterations.
2. Verify your `results/*.json` files exist.
3. Open a PR adding your row, attach raw results to `submissions/<provider>-<model>/<date>/`.
4. Or open an Issue with results — maintainers can re-run for verification.
